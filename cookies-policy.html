Cookie Policy

Last Updated: January 27, 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how Inuka na Ploti ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website at inukanaploti.co.ke (the "Website"). This policy explains what these technologies are, why we use them, and your rights to control our use of them.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

Cookies allow websites to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Improve your browsing experience
  • Understand how you use the website
  • Provide personalized content and advertisements

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for several reasons:

1. Essential Cookies (Always Active)

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic features like page navigation, security features, and access to secure areas of the website. The website cannot function properly without these cookies.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
cookie_consent Stores your cookie consent preferences 1 year

2. Analytics Cookies (Optional)

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. This helps us improve our website and services.

_ga Used to distinguish users (Google Analytics) 2 years

_gat Used to throttle request rate (Google Analytics) 1 minute

3. Marketing Cookies (Optional)

These cookies are used to track visitors across websites. They are used to display ads that are relevant and engaging for individual users.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
_fbp Used by Facebook for advertising and analytics 3 months

Your Cookie Choices

You have several options for managing cookies:

1. Cookie Consent Banner

When you first visit our website, you'll see a cookie consent banner. You can choose to:

  • Accept All: Allow all cookies including analytics and marketing
  • Decline: Only essential cookies will be used
  • Cookie Settings: Choose which types of cookies you want to allow

2. Manage Cookie Preferences

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by clicking the button below:

Manage Cookie Preferences

3. Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • View what cookies are stored and delete them individually
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block all cookies from specific websites
  • Block all cookies from being set
  • Delete all cookies when you close your browser

Note: If you choose to block all cookies, you may not be able to access all or parts of our website.

Browser-Specific Instructions:

  • Google Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Mozilla Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

Third-Party Cookies

In addition to our own cookies, we may also use various third-party cookies to:

  • Track and analyze website usage (Google Analytics)
  • Provide social media features (Facebook, Twitter, WhatsApp)
  • Deliver relevant advertisements

These third parties may use cookies to collect information about your online activities across different websites over time.

Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Cookie Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
