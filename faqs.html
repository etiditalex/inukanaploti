General Questions

Inuka na Ploti is a trusted land investment company in Kenya that specializes in providing premium land investment opportunities with flexible payment plans. We focus on prime locations in Kilifi, Mtwapa, and Malindi, offering guaranteed title deeds and transparent processes.

Our properties are strategically located in prime areas of Kenya, including:

  • Kilifi - Coastal region with beach access
  • Mtwapa - Growing commercial area
  • Malindi - Tourist destination with high potential
  • Bofa - Emerging residential area
  • Vipingo - Golf course and resort area

All our properties come with guaranteed title deeds and are fully documented. We provide:

  • Original title deeds
  • Land registry verification
  • Survey maps and beacons
  • Legal documentation
  • Site visits for verification

Payment & Financing

We offer three flexible payment plans:

  • Standard Plan: 20% deposit, 12-24 months to complete
  • Extended Plan: 15% deposit, 24-36 months to complete
  • Premium Plan: 10% deposit, up to 48 months to complete

All plans have no interest charges and flexible monthly payments.

No, there are no hidden fees or charges. The price you see is the price you pay. We believe in complete transparency and include all costs in our quoted prices. The only additional cost is the standard government registration fee for the title deed transfer, which is typically around Ksh 1,000-2,000.

Yes, all our properties can be paid for in installments. You can choose from our flexible payment plans that range from 12 to 48 months. Monthly payments are calculated based on the remaining balance after your initial deposit, divided by your chosen payment period.

We understand that financial situations can change. If you miss a payment, we will contact you to discuss your situation and work out a solution. We offer payment extensions and can adjust your payment schedule if needed. Our goal is to help you complete your investment successfully.

Title Deeds

You receive your title deed immediately upon completion of your final payment. We process the title deed transfer within 30 days of your last payment, ensuring you have full legal ownership of your property.

Yes, all our title deeds are genuine and legally registered. We provide:

  • Original title deeds from the land registry
  • Land registry verification
  • Survey maps and beacons
  • Legal documentation
  • Government registration certificates

Yes, once you have your title deed, you have full ownership rights and can sell, transfer, or develop your property as you wish. There are no restrictions on your ownership rights.

Site Visits & Inspection

Absolutely! We encourage all potential buyers to visit the property before making a decision. We provide:

  • Guided site visits
  • Property inspection
  • Location verification
  • Infrastructure assessment
  • Neighborhood tour

Contact us to schedule a site visit at your convenience.

During your site visit, pay attention to:

  • Property boundaries and beacons
  • Access roads and infrastructure
  • Water and electricity availability
  • Surrounding developments
  • Security and safety
  • Future development plans

Legal & Process

To purchase a property, you need:

  • National ID or passport
  • KRA PIN certificate
  • Proof of income (payslip or bank statement)
  • Two passport photos
  • Contact information

We handle all the legal paperwork and documentation for you.

The purchase process typically takes 2-4 weeks from the time you make your initial deposit. This includes:

  • Document verification (3-5 days)
  • Legal processing (1-2 weeks)
  • Title deed preparation (1 week)
  • Final handover (1-2 days)

Yes, we offer a 7-day cooling-off period from the date of your initial deposit. During this time, you can cancel your purchase and receive a full refund if you change your mind.
