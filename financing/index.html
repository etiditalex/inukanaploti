Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our financing options and payment plans.

What is the minimum deposit required?

Our minimum deposit is 10% of the property value, making it accessible for most investors.

Are there any hidden fees?

No hidden fees. The only additional costs are standard legal fees for title transfer, which we handle transparently.

Can I pay off my property early?

Yes! We offer early payment discounts and you can pay off your property at any time without penalties.

What happens if I miss a payment?

We understand financial challenges. Contact us to discuss flexible payment arrangements and avoid any issues.

When do I get my title deed?

Your title deed is processed and transferred to you immediately upon completion of all payments.

Can I change my payment plan?

Yes, you can upgrade or modify your payment plan at any time by contacting our team.
